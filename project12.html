Project

YAN London

YAN LONDON WAS FOUNDED IN 2018 AS A DEDICATION TO MY SON AFTER WITNESSING HIS COURAGEOUS WILL TO FIGHT THROUGH COMPLICATIONS AT BIRTH.

OUR OBJECTIVE IS TO CREATE AN INSPIRED GLOBAL COMMUNITY BUILT UP BY INFLUENCERS THAT SHARE OUR CORE BELIEFS:

COURAGE – PERSEVERANCE – INFLUENCE

OUR LOGO IS THE EMBODIMENT OF THE FIGHTING SPIRIT WE ALL POSSESS & WHEN USED CORRECTLY IT CAN PROPEL US TO GREATNESS.

IF WE CAN INSPIRE ONE PERSON TO DO THIS THEN WE HAVE MADE A DIFFERENCE TO BE PROUD OF.

YAN LONDON EXISTS AFTER WITNESSING MY SON’S COURAGEOUS WILL TO FIGHT THROUGH COMPLICATIONS AT BIRTH. UNABLE TO BREATHE ON HIS OWN HE WAS PLACED INTO INTENSIVE CARE. I RECALL SEEING HIM IN AN INCUBATOR WITH BREATHING APPARATUS AND MONITORING EQUIPMENT ATTACHED TO HIM. IT WASN’T HOW THINGS WERE MEANT TO BE, YET HERE WE WERE. TRYING MY HARDEST TO FIGHT BACK TEARS AS I STOOD BESIDE HIM, I WAS PRAYING HE WOULD GAIN STRENGTH & RECOVER.
