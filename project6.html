Project

Mistico Park

HAVE THE TIME OF YOUR LIFE AT MISTICO PARK.

Mistico Arenal Hanging Bridges Park is a biological reserve, subject to the forestry law of the Republic of Costa Rica. We intend to have as many people as possible enjoy the beauties found inside this protected forest. However, because it is fragile, it is our duty to oversee and control access and visitor behavior, in order to minimize the impact on the flora and fauna. Therefore, it is essential that visitors abide by the following regulations:

1. For your safety please stay on the trail. Enjoy safely, jumping, rocking or running is not allowed on hanging bridges.

2. In order to not disturb the nature structured systems, please do not take any wildlife from the park.

3. Access to the park with food is not allowed.

4. The maximum number of people allowed on the bridge at the same time is 15. For your security please do not exceed it.

5. With your visit to Mistico Park you will collaborate directly in sponsorships of social programs and local charities and environmental education institutions.
