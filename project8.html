Modi Health

ModiHealth is a virtual health and wellness company that takes an integrative approach to health, providing virtual care solutions to improve access to care, patient outcomes, and quality of life.

ModiHealth is the only platform to offer both live telemedicine and tele-wellness consultations as well as tools and support to optimize health and well-being through our on demand health programs and our health and wellness streaming service. All care on our platform is based on an integrative, whole-person approach. Built for practitioners by practitioners, ModiHealth offers every type of care under one virtual roof, empowering clients and patients to take charge of their own unique health and wellness journeys while enabling practitioners to offer an integrative, team approach to quality care.
