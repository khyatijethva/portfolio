National Tire & Wheel

We would like to thank you for taking the time to visit our online catalog and store. Your satisfaction, trust and business are very important to us.

National Tire & Wheel has been in the off-road and four wheel drive business for well over 30 years. When we first got the itch to go play off-road, lift kits were almost nonexistent. The largest tires available were 12x15s and names like Gumbo Mudder, Ground Hawg, Armstrong Tru Tracs, and Norsemans were the way to go. Radial off-road tires hadn't even been thought about yet. Jackman white spoke wheels were the hot set-up unless you really wanted to be cool and got yourself a set of the Western Wheel Cyclones.

We have been in the business for many years, we have a 100,000+ sq ft warehouse packed with merchandise and we have experienced sales people and a toll free number that you can call and get your questions answered.

We are here to take care of you during and after the sale, you can count on that!
