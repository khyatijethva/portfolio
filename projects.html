Project

Le miro house

Located in an incomparable environment, to enjoy nature, relax and tranquility.

This totally revamped residental building is located in Palma’s central and upcoming district of El Terreno. The whole area is undergoing extensive regeneration and is predicted to be one of Palma’s new hotspot. The area has popular Santa Catalina as a near neighbour and is close to the Bellver forest, a large area of natural beauty. El Terreno occupies the slope that rises up from the harbour side promenade of Paseo Maritimo, with Palma city center just a 15 minutes stroll away.

Showroom: Joan Maragall 17- G 07006 Palma, Illes Balears
